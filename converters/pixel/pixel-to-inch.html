Pixel to Inch Conversion Instructions

In digital devices, 1 inch is typically equal to 96 pixels. This standard DPI (pixels per inch) is widely used in most modern display devices.

Common Conversion Values Reference:

  • 96px = 1in
  • 48px = 0.5in
  • 192px = 2in
  • 24px = 0.25in
