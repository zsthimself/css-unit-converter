Inch to Feet Conversion Instructions

In the imperial unit system, 1 foot equals 12 inches. This is a standard conversion relationship commonly used in construction and engineering measurements.

Common conversion values:

  • 12in = 1ft
  • 24in = 2ft
  • 6in = 0.5ft
  • 36in = 3ft (1码)
