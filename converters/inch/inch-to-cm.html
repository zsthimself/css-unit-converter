Inch to Centimeter Conversion Instructions

Inch (in) is a unit of length in the imperial system, and 1 inch is precisely equal to 2.54 centimeters. This is a fixed conversion relationship, unaffected by devices or displays.

Common conversion values reference:

  • 1in = 2.54cm
  • 2in = 5.08cm
  • 0.5in = 1.27cm
  • 12in = 30.48cm (1 foot)
