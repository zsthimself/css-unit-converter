What are Viewport Height (VH) and Points (PT)?

Viewport height (VH) is a percentage unit relative to the height of the viewport. Points (PT) are commonly used units in printing and typesetting.

Usage Instructions

  1. Enter the viewport height value to convert
  2. Click the "Convert" button or press Enter to get the result
  3. Use the "Copy" button to copy the conversion result

Common Conversion Examples

  • 1vh = 0.75pt (assuming viewport height is 1000px)
  • 2vh = 1.5pt
